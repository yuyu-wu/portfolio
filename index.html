About Me

I had my daughter three years ago, and watching a child grow is like experiencing the world all over again. They explore all the time, and constant trial and error is their daily life. Inspired by her I decided to try something new myself and started to learn web development.

Before my journey as a developer I was an interpreter. To assist communication, interpreters have to understand the topic themselves, and there is always a new area to dive into. I loved the thrill to always face a challenge and learn new things as you go.
